The largest ribosomal RNA contigs are the basis for the substitution analysis. The following HTML pages were produced for manual inspection of results by a script marking in black positions disagreeing with the consensus. To see the assembly results inspect the contigs with no cleaning (referred to by 'end0'). To ignore single misassembled reads, as well as problematic read ends, the following cleaning procedure was applied. Start counting after 5 (end5) consecutive bases are identical to the consensus and ignore reads with more than 5 (rm5)changes compared to the consensus.

Warning: Streptomycinae largest contigs are HUGE, file load slowly and scrolling is very slow too!
